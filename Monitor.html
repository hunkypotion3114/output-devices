A monitor is made up of circuitry, a screen, a power supply, buttons to adjust screen settings, and casing that holds all of these components.Like early TVs, the first monitors were comprised of a cathode ray tube and a fluorescent screen. Today, all monitors are have flat-panel display technology, usually backlit with LEDs.The first computer monitor was part of the Xerox Alto computer system, which was released on March 1, 1973.
