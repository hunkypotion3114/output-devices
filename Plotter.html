Plotter

Plotters can work on very large sheets of paper while maintaining high resolution. They can print on a wide variety of flat materials including plywood, aluminum, sheet steel, cardboard, and plastic. Plotters allow the same pattern to be drawn thousands of times without any image degradation. The first plotter was invented in 1953 by Remington-Rand. It was used in conjunction with the UNIVAC computer to created technical drawings.
