Printer

The write kind of output device

Printers are one of the most popular computer peripherals and are commonly used to print text and photos.The first person to invent a mechanical printing device is Charles Babbage, who created it with his Difference Engine in the 1800s.Chuck Hull created the 3D printer in 1984. 3D printers work by taking a digital blueprint of an object and reproducing it layer-by-layer using various materials such as plastic and metal alloys.
