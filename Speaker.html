Speakers

Bringing the vibe to every party

A computer speaker connects to a computer to generate sound. The signal used to produce the sound is created by the computer's sound card. Speakers are made up of a cone, an iron coil, a magnet, and housing (case). When the speaker receives electrical input from a device, it sends the current through causing it to move back and forth. This motion then vibrates the outer cone, generating sound waves picked up by our ears.
